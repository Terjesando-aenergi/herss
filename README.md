HERSS (The Hydraulic Economic River System Simulator) is a simulation tool for hydropower
river systems, modeling reservoirs, power stations, and channels connected in a river
network. It computes the physical water balance and the associated economic value
(production, cost, and revenue) of a given operational strategy over a chosen time
horizon. HERSS is designed to be driven by external optimization algorithms, which can
call it repeatedly to search for water-value-maximizing operational strategies subject
to physical and regulatory constraints such as minimum environmental flow requirements.
